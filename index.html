College Core Values

George Brown College is a college offering degree programs in a variety of fields. It is known for its hands-on approach to learning and provides students with internship and professional development opportunities.

Inclusition

George Brown College is committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment that respects diversity, equity, and justice. The institution recognizes the importance of representing different cultures, perspectives, and experiences. The university values ​​the contribution that each student and employee can make to the community. All college initiatives are aimed at ensuring equal opportunities for all.

Professionalism

Our team strives to develop high professional standards and skills in students that meet the requirements of the real labor market. An important aspect is preparing students for a successful career and employment. The college also values ​​ethical behavior and responsibility. Teaching is focused on providing knowledge and experience that will help students be competitive.

Innovations

The College encourages students and faculty to continually seek new and original solutions. It is a place where students develop critical thinking and innovative ideas. Each individual is expected to strive for creative problem solving. We support a culture of experimentation and adaptation to modern requirements.

Available college tools and spaces

Our college offers a wide range of tools and workspaces that make working with us particularly convenient.

OUR LOCATIONS IN TORONTO

Casa Loma Campus

The Casa Loma Campus is one of the key locations of George Brown College, situated in downtown Toronto. It offers a variety of programs, especially in the fields of health sciences, community services, and early childhood education. The campus is known for its modern facilities, student focused approach, and proximity to both local businesses and community services. It's an ideal place for students seeking a dynamic and supportive learning environment.

St. James Campus

The St. James Campus is another central hub of George Brown College, located in downtown Toronto. It is home to a wide range of programs, particularly in business, hospitality, and creative arts. The campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including specialized labs and studios, and provides students with practical, hands-on learning experiences. Its vibrant downtown location offers students access to numerous career opportunities and professional networks.

Waterfront Campus

The Waterfront Campus of George Brown College is a modern facility located along Toronto’s waterfront. It offers programs in health sciences, design, and business, with a focus on innovation and hands-on learning. The campus features open, state-of-the-art spaces, providing a vibrant environment for students to thrive. Its scenic location and cutting-edge resources make it an ideal space for both learning and professional development.

Lower Jar. Campus

The 3 Lower Jarvis campus of George Brown College is a dynamic space focused on providing hands-on training in technology and applied arts. With modern facilities and specialized labs, it offers programs that prepare students for careers in fields like construction, engineering, and design. Its central location provides easy access to industry hubs and real-world opportunities.
